sherpa is a service that allows you to use email to post comments to message threads on BasecampTM using any mobile email device like a Treo, Blackberry or Sidekick.

How does it work?

When you signup, you tell sherpa your BasecampTM url and login information. sherpa stores this information in an encrypted database and does not send this information via email. sherpa uses this information to allow you to login to your BasecampTM account via the email messages you send from your mobile email device.

sherpa creates a unique email address for you to use when communicating with your BasecampTM account via your mobile email device. To comment on message threads from BasecampTM via email from your mobile device, you simply forward your comments to the unique sherpa email address.

For example, you receive a comment alert from a message thread on BasecampTM. instead of clicking on the url in the message to post a reply, you simply forward that comment alert email, with your new comments, to your unique sherpa address and sherpa does the rest. sherpa receives your email and posts your comments on the appropriate thread on BasecampTM. You never have to visit the BasecampTM site, but instead you run BasecampTM via email from your mobile device.

What does it cost?

Sherpa is free to try for 30 days. After 30 days, you pay only $20 for one year. You can cancel at anytime with no further obligation.
